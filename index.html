M.S.Dhoni

The Captain Cool

Here's a short summary of Mahi's exploits:

Mahendra Singh Dhoni popularly known as M.S.Dhoni was nicknamed captain cool, his level headedness and calm composure in trying situations earned him that nickname.

He was the captain of the 2011 world cup winning squad.

He is one of the most successful cricket captain ever.
He was the best wicketkeeper batsman during his captainship days.

He is revered among youngsters for his amazing keeping skills on the field.
The helicopter shot is his signature shot that he used against one of the most feared bowler in cricket history, Sri Lankan bowler, Lasith Malinga.
